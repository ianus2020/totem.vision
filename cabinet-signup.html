Eligibility Criteria:
Users must be at least 18 years old to participate in the Totem ICO. Users must comply with all local laws and regulations regarding cryptocurrency investments.

KYC/AML Compliance:
Users are required to complete a Know Your Customer (KYC) verification process, which includes submitting valid identification documents. Users must comply with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations to ensure the legitimacy of their funds.
